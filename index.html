I'm a recent University of Guelph graduate with a Bachelor of Arts in Culture and Technology Studies — a program at the intersection of digital methods, critical thinking, and creative practice.

I’m drawn to work that integrates both communication and design together. I love crafting content that connects, designing visuals that communicate with clarity, and building digital experiences that are purposeful, intuitive, and engaging.

My academic background gave me a foundation in understanding how technology shapes culture, and how design and communication shape the way we experience technology in return. I'm now bringing that perspective into professional work, with a focus on communications, digital design, and web content roles.

Based in Barrie, Ontario, and open to remote and hybrid opportunities.
